Your Journey With PwaniStays

Follow these simple steps from inquiry to arrival

1

Explore & Inquire

Browse our curated collection of properties and experiences, or simply tell us about your travel plans and preferences.

  • Browse accommodation & experiences online
  • Contact us with dates, group size & preferences
  • Request custom recommendations
  • No obligation initial consultation
2

Personal Consultation

Our Kenya experts review your request and provide tailored recommendations based on location, comfort, and budget.

  • Personalized property/experience matches
  • Honest advice on locations & amenities
  • Detailed information & photos shared
  • Transparent pricing breakdown
3

Confirm Your Choice

Select your preferred option and we'll confirm availability while providing all necessary details before any payment.

  • Real-time availability confirmation
  • Complete booking details shared
  • Clear terms & conditions
  • Secure payment options presented
4

Secure Payment & Visa Assistance

Complete your booking with secure payment options and let us handle your Kenya eTA visa application.

  • Multiple secure payment methods
  • eTA visa application service ($90)
  • Official confirmation & receipts
  • Travel document assistance
5

Arrival & Continuous Support

Receive detailed arrival information and enjoy local support throughout your stay in Kenya.

  • Detailed check-in instructions
  • Local contact & emergency numbers
  • On-ground support throughout stay
  • Assistance with any needs
