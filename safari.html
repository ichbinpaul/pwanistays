Best Time for Safari

Plan your safari according to Kenya's wildlife seasons

January - March

Dry season, great for general wildlife viewing. Pleasant weather with clear skies.

April - May

Long rains, fewer tourists, lush green landscapes. Lower rates available.

June - October

Peak season. Great Migration in Masai Mara. Dry weather, excellent game viewing.

November - December

Short rains, good wildlife viewing. Migratory birds arrive. Holiday season.
