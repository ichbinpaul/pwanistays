Our Story

From a simple idea to Kenya's trusted coastal hospitality partner

PwaniStays was founded on the belief that coastal travel should be seamless, authentic, and memorable. Born from a partnership between local Kenyan expertise and international travel experience, we set out to bridge the gap between travelers and the authentic beauty of Kenya's coastline.

Our journey began with recognizing that many travelers miss out on genuine coastal experiences due to lack of local knowledge or unreliable services. We saw an opportunity to create a platform that not only offers quality accommodation but also provides the local insights and support that make travel truly special.

Today, PwaniStays has grown into a comprehensive coastal hospitality service, offering curated stays, seamless transfers, and authentic experiences across Mombasa, Diani, Watamu, and beyond. Every property we list is personally verified, every service provider is vetted, and every guest receives the attention and care that has become our hallmark.

We believe that great travel isn't just about where you stay, but how you experience a destination. That's why we focus on creating journeys that are not just trips, but memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission & Vision

The guiding principles that drive everything we do at PwaniStays

Our Mission
